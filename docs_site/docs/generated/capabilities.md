# Capabilities

## `number_bonds_to_10`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `7`
- recommended level: Year 2

Recall pairs of numbers that make 10 without counting.

Success criteria: Answers 10 bond questions with confidence in under 2 minutes.

## `addition_facts_to_20`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `7`
- recommended level: Year 2

Solve one-digit and teen addition facts within 20 accurately.

Success criteria: Scores at least 8 out of 10 on a mixed addition drill.

## `subtraction_facts_to_20`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `7`
- recommended level: Year 2

Solve subtraction facts within 20 using number sense instead of slow counting.

Success criteria: Completes a mixed subtraction drill with at least 80 percent accuracy.

## `times_tables_2_5_10`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `10`
- recommended level: Year 5

Recall multiplication facts for the 2, 5, and 10 tables quickly.

Success criteria: Answers at least 12 multiplication facts correctly in one session.

## `times_tables_3_4`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `10`
- recommended level: Year 5

Build accurate recall for the 3 and 4 times tables.

Success criteria: Scores at least 9 out of 12 on a mixed 3 and 4 tables check.

## `division_facts_by_2_5_10`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `10`
- recommended level: Year 5

Use inverse multiplication knowledge to solve division facts by 2, 5, and 10.

Success criteria: Solves 10 inverse-fact questions with at least 80 percent accuracy.

## `division_facts_by_3_4`

- subject: `maths`
- recommended age: `10`
- recommended level: Year 5

Apply tables knowledge to solve division facts by 3 and 4.

Success criteria: Scores at least 8 out of 10 on a mixed division check.

## `read_aloud_level_1`

- subject: `english`
- recommended age: `7`
- recommended level: Year 2

Read a short age-appropriate text with basic fluency and expression.

Success criteria: Reads a short passage aloud with clear pacing and no more than a few prompts.
