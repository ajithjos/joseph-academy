# Evidence And Inference Questions

Use the passage "How Bees Share Information".

## Summarise with evidence

1. Summarise the first paragraph in one sentence.
2. Copy one phrase that shows what the waggle dance communicates.
3. Summarise the second paragraph in one sentence.
4. Copy one detail that shows why the message can be harder to follow.

## Inference questions

1. Why might several workers watch more than one dancer before leaving the hive?
2. What does the final paragraph suggest about why shared signals matter?
3. Does the passage present bees as random or organised? Explain with one text clue.
