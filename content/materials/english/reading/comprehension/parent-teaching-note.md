# Parent Teaching Note For Reading Comprehension With Evidence

Push for accurate thinking, not impressive-sounding guesses.

## Look for

- whether the learner can separate the main idea from an interesting detail
- whether an answer points back to a real line or phrase in the passage
- whether an inference stays plausible instead of drifting into imagination

## Good next moves

- ask "which words made you think that?"
- insist on one short summary before a longer explanation
- reread only the paragraph that matters instead of restarting the whole passage every time
