# Parent Teaching Note For Reading Fluency And Retell

Aim for accurate, meaningful reading, not dramatic performance.

## Look for

- whether decoding mistakes break the meaning of the sentence
- whether the learner pauses at sensible points
- whether the retell keeps the order instead of listing random details

## Good next moves

- reread the same short passage before switching texts
- correct one stubborn word and then return to the sentence
- ask "what happened next?" when the retell gets stuck
