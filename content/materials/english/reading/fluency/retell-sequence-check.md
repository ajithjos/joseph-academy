# Retell Sequence Check

Use the passage "Echo At The Park".

## Say or write

1. What happened first?
2. What did the children notice next?
3. Why did they move away from the path?
4. How did the game change at the end?

Retell challenge:

- Give the whole story in four clear sentences.
- Keep the events in the right order.
