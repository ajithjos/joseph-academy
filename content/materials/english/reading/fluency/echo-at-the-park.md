# Echo At The Park

Mina and Joel raced to the park before breakfast. The swings were still wet from last night's rain, so they ran to the climbing frame instead. Joel shouted from the top platform, and Mina heard his voice bounce back from the brick wall near the gate. They tried it again with different words until they could hear a clear echo each time.

Then Mina noticed a grounds worker rolling a hose across the path. "We should move," she said. The children stepped aside, waited for the path to clear, and then made up a new game. They took turns calling out one sentence and listening for the last word to return.

Read the passage aloud twice.

- First read: aim for accuracy.
- Second read: aim for smoother phrasing and clearer pauses.
