# Parent Teaching Note For Tables And Division

Look for patterns rather than isolated errors.

## Ask

- Does the learner know the multiplication fact but hesitate on the division inverse?
- Are errors clustered around one table, such as 4s?
- Is the learner rushing and dropping signs or numbers?

## Good next moves

- repeat one weak table with small bursts
- pair multiplication and division in the same review
- finish with one success item the learner can answer confidently
