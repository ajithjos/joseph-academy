# Parent Teaching Note For Mental Arithmetic Fluency

Keep the pace brisk, but do not confuse speed with panic.

## Look for

- instant recall on bond facts
- whether the learner naturally makes the next ten
- whether subtraction errors come from weak bonds or from losing track of the bridge

## Good next moves

- reteach one weak pattern with three or four examples, not a long lecture
- mix one easy fact into each harder set so confidence stays honest
- stop once attention drops instead of forcing volume for its own sake
