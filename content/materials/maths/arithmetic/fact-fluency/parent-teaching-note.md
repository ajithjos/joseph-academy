# Parent Teaching Note For Multiplication And Division Fact Fluency

This stage is the foundation for later written multiplication and division. Do not rush past it.

## Look for

- whether the learner recalls the multiplication fact directly or still counts repeatedly
- whether division answers come from known inverse facts rather than guessing
- whether missing-number equations are solved by relationship or by trial and error

## Good next moves

- keep one weak table in daily review until it becomes automatic
- pair each multiplication fact with its matching division fact in the same session
- stop and reteach the relationship if the learner can say facts but cannot use the fact family
