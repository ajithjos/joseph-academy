# Multiplication Facts Foundations

This playlist is the normal multiplication entry point once addition and subtraction facts are reasonably secure.

The learner should see multiplication as equal groups and scaling before being asked to perform fast mixed recall.

## Completion Rule

Move on when `2`, `5`, and `10` facts are strong in mixed order and division prompts no longer feel like a separate topic.
