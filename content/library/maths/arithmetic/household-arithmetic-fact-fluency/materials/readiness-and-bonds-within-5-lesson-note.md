# Readiness And Bonds Within 5 Lesson Note

Look fast. Say the whole. Then say the parts.

## Small groups

- `1` is one thing.
- `2` can be `1 and 1`.
- `3` can be `2 and 1`.
- `4` can be `2 and 2` or `3 and 1`.
- `5` can be `4 and 1` or `3 and 2`.

## Pairs that make 5

- `0 and 5 make 5`
- `1 and 4 make 5`
- `2 and 3 make 5`
- `3 and 2 make 5`
- `4 and 1 make 5`
- `5 and 0 make 5`

## Tiny take-away facts

- `5 - 1 = 4`
- `5 - 2 = 3`
- `4 - 1 = 3`
- `3 - 1 = 2`

If you know the whole and the parts, you already know the addition and take-away fact.

Come back to this note until the pairs to `5` feel obvious.
