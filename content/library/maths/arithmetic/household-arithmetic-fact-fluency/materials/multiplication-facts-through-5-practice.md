2. `4 x 5 =`

9. `4 x 4 =`
10. `2 x 4 =`
11. `5 x 2 =`
12. `4 x 3 =`
13. `3 x 2 =`

## Part C: Explain the pattern

15. `3 x 4 =` and explain which easier fact helped you
16. `4 x 3 =` and explain how doubling can help
