# Readiness And Bonds Within 5 Teaching Note

Use counters, buttons, or fingers.

1. Show a group of up to 5 objects for two seconds, then hide it and ask, `How many did you see?`
2. Split 5 into two parts and say the whole fact aloud, such as `3 and 2 make 5`.
3. Ask the inverse prompt straight away, such as `If 3 and 2 make 5, what is 5 take away 2?`
4. Stop before fatigue appears. A short calm session is better than a long frustrated one.
