# Addition And Subtraction Facts To 10 Lesson Note

Know the pair and you know the whole fact family.

## Bonds to 10

- `1 and 9 make 10`
- `2 and 8 make 10`
- `3 and 7 make 10`
- `4 and 6 make 10`
- `5 and 5 make 10`

## Fact families

If `7 + 3 = 10`, then:

- `3 + 7 = 10`
- `10 - 7 = 3`
- `10 - 3 = 7`

If `6 + 2 = 8`, then:

- `2 + 6 = 8`
- `8 - 6 = 2`
- `8 - 2 = 6`

## What to notice

- Addition builds the whole.
- Subtraction tells you the missing part.
- If you freeze, return to the nearest bond you already know.

Read the pair first. Then say the matching subtraction.
