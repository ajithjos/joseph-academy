# Readiness And Bonds Within 5

This stage prepares a younger or less secure learner for the rest of the pathway.

## Plain-Language Meaning

The learner can see and say small number relationships without recounting every time.

## Skills In This Stage

- `count_small_groups_within_5`
- `number_bonds_within_5`
- `add_and_subtract_within_5`

## Secure Performance

The learner recognises small groups quickly, gives pairs within 5, and handles very small add-and-take-away facts without strain.
