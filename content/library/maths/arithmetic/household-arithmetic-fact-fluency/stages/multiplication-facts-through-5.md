# Multiplication Facts Through 5

This stage broadens early multiplication into short mixed work through `5 x 5`.

## Plain-Language Meaning

The learner can answer mixed facts with factors up to 5 without needing to recite the whole table aloud first.

## Skills In This Stage

- `multiply_by_3_and_4`
- `mixed_multiplication_facts_through_5x5`

## Secure Performance

The learner answers short mixed sets through `5 x 5` accurately and does not freeze when prompts are shuffled.
