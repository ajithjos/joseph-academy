# Subtract Two And Three Digit Numbers

- successful performance: subtracts two-digit and three-digit numbers accurately and uses exchange only when needed
- likely mistakes: subtracts the smaller digit from the larger regardless of place, or exchanges without understanding the trade
- out of scope: negative results and decimals
