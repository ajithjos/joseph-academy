# Interpret Multiplication As Arrays And Scaling

- successful performance: explains multiplication as equal groups, arrays, or scaling and chooses a sensible representation for the calculation
- likely mistakes: treats multiplication as a symbol change without understanding the structure of the groups
- out of scope: fractions or algebraic scaling
