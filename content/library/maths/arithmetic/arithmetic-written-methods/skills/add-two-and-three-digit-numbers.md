# Add Two And Three Digit Numbers

- successful performance: adds two-digit and three-digit numbers accurately with correct alignment and sensible exchange
- likely mistakes: misaligns columns, forgets an exchange, or writes the carried value in the wrong place
- out of scope: decimals and algebraic notation
