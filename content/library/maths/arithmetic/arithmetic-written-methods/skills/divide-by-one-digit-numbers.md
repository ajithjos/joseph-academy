# Divide By One Digit Numbers

- successful performance: divides numbers by one-digit divisors accurately, handles simple remainders, and checks with multiplication
- likely mistakes: stops too early, loses place value, or gives a remainder larger than the divisor
- out of scope: long division with multi-digit divisors or decimal quotients
