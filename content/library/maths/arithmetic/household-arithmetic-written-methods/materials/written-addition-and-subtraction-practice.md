## Part C: Explain the exchange

9. In `47 + 36`, why do you exchange after adding the ones?
10. In `83 - 27`, what happened to one ten?
11. In `704 - 268`, why is more than one exchange needed?

## Part D: Error check

12. A learner says `47 + 36 = 713` because `7 + 6 = 13` and `4 + 3 = 7`. What did they misunderstand?
13. A learner lines up `395 + 87` with the `8` under the `3`. Why is that wrong?
