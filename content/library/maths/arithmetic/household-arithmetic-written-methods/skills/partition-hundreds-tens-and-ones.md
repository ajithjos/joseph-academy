# Partition Hundreds Tens And Ones

- successful performance: partitions numbers into hundreds, tens, and ones and can recombine them accurately
- likely mistakes: treats digits as separate counts without place-value meaning or swaps tens and ones
- out of scope: decimals or algebraic partitioning
