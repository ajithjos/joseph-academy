# Interpret Division As Sharing And Grouping

- successful performance: explains division as sharing into equal parts or grouping into equal sets and can tell what a remainder means
- likely mistakes: mixes up the number of groups with the size of each group or ignores the remainder entirely
- out of scope: fractions and long division with large divisors
