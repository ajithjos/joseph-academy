# Multiply By One Digit Numbers

- successful performance: multiplies two-digit and three-digit numbers by one-digit numbers using partitioning or a short written method and can explain the parts
- likely mistakes: ignores place value inside the larger number or loses track of partial products
- out of scope: two-digit by two-digit multiplication
