# Addition And Subtraction Facts To 20 Practice

Work in mixed order. Use a bridge through `10` when it helps.

## Part A: Make 20 or find the missing part

1. `9 + __ = 20`
2. `__ + 7 = 20`
3. `20 - __ = 4`
4. `12 + __ = 20`

6. `20 - 7 =`
7. `16 - 8 =`
8. `11 + 6 =`
9. `18 - 9 =`
10. `14 + 5 =`
11. `17 - 6 =`

13. `15 - 7 =`
