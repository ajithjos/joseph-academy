# Multiplication Facts Through 10 Practice

Work in mixed order. Rebuild the hard facts from an anchor when you need to.

1. `6 x 7 =`
2. `8 x 4 =`
3. `9 x 6 =`
4. `7 x 8 =`

## Part B: Mixed multiplication and division

5. `42 / 7 =`
6. `54 / 6 =`
7. `9 x 9 =`
8. `56 / 8 =`
9. `7 x 6 =`
10. `63 / 9 =`
11. `8 x 7 =`
12. `72 / 8 =`
13. `6 x 8 =`
14. `49 / 7 =`

## Part C: Explain the rebuild

15. `7 x 6 =` and explain the easier facts you used
16. `9 x 8 =` and explain the `10 x` strategy

Coach check: if the learner freezes on one cluster such as the `7` facts, practise that cluster with the anchor aloud before returning to full mixed work.
