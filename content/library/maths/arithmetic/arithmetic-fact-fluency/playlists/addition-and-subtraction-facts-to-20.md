# Addition And Subtraction Facts To 20

Use this playlist when facts to 10 are reliable but teen-number combinations, missing-number prompts, and subtraction through 20 still slow the learner down.

The main teaching move here is to stop the learner from counting every teen fact step by step. The learner should start spotting a known bond inside the larger fact.

## Completion Rule

Move on when the learner can handle mixed facts through 20 with only occasional counting, can solve missing-number prompts sensibly, and can bridge through 10 without needing the full count each time.
