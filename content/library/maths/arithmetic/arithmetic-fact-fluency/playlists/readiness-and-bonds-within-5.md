# Readiness And Bonds Within 5

Use this playlist for younger learners or for any older learner who still treats tiny number relationships as fresh counting problems every time.

The teaching goal is simple: the learner should start seeing small amounts as a whole with parts inside, not as a chain of ones to recount.

## Completion Rule

Move on only when the learner can answer most pairs to 5 quickly, recognise small groups without recounting, and handle tiny add-and-take-away prompts without visible strain.

If the learner is older, do not skip this just because it looks easy on paper. If bonds within 5 are shaky, later facts stay shaky too.
