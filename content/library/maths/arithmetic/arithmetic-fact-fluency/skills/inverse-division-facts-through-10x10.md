# Inverse Division Facts Through 10 x 10

- successful performance: works back from known multiplication facts to answer short division prompts through `10 x 10`
- likely mistakes: memorises multiplication but freezes when the same fact is asked in inverse form
- out of scope: long division procedures or remainders
