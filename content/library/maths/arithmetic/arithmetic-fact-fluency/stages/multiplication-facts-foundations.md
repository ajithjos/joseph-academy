# Multiplication Facts Foundations

This stage establishes the easiest and most useful multiplication patterns first.

## Plain-Language Meaning

The learner becomes reliable on `2`, `5`, and `10` facts and starts seeing the linked division relationships.

## Skills In This Stage

- `multiply_by_2_5_10`
- `related_division_facts_for_2_5_10`

## Secure Performance

The learner answers grouped and mixed facts for `2`, `5`, and `10` quickly and can work back from the matching division fact.
